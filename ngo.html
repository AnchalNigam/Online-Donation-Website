Associate NGO's

Sarthak Foundation is Not-For-Profit organisation.It is registered under the Societies Act 1860, and are 12A and 80G (Income Tax Exemption) certified.It's mission is to To serve children on their path to more fulfilling life by empowering them, in realizing their worth and potential, through support, opportunity and education; thereby contributing to a stronger community and healthier world.



HUMSAFAR, a Support Centre for Women in Crisis was set up in Lucknow in November 2003 to ensure a holistic response to women's gender and human rights violations and to work towards gender equality. Registered as a Trust in Lucknow 2008,it has been collectively managed by a group of Trustees,a large number of vigilant volunteers in communities and educational establishment and professionals.




Pratham is an innovative learning organization created to improve the quality of education in India. As one of the largest non-governmental organizations in the country, Pratham focuses on high-quality, low-cost, and replicable interventions to address gaps in the education system. Established in 1995 to provide education to children in the slums of Mumbai, Pratham has grown both in scope and geographical coverage.




The Dada-Dadi is a non-profit service and advocacy organization headquartered at Chandigarh India. Registered with Registrar of Societies at Chandigarh, India. Dada-Dadi brings together non-profit organizations, businesses and government to develop creative solutions that improve the lives of all older adults.




National Handicapped Finance and Development Corporation (NHFDC) was set up in January 1997 as a non profit company under Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India. The corporation provides financial assistance for wide range of income generating activities to disabled persons. The company is managed by Board of Directors nominated by Government of India.
